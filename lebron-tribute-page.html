The Highlights Of LeBron James' Basketball Career:

  • 1984 - Originally Born in Akron, Ohio
  • 1999 - Attended High School at St. Vincent-St. Mary High School
  • 2003 - Decided Not to go to college and was drafted #1 in the NBA Draft to the Cleveland Cavaliers
  • 2010 - After playing in his home town for 7 seasons he became a free agent and made "The Decision" where he told the world he would be playing for the Miami Heat
  • 2012 & 2013 - Won Championships with the Heat
  • 2014 - LeBron went back to his hometown team the Cleveland
  • 2016 - LeBron finally won a Championship for his hometown Cleveland Cavaliers
  • 2018 - LeBron became a free agent again and made the decision to go to the Los Angeles Lakers
  • 2020 - LeBron won another Championship with the Los Angeles Lakers.
  • Now - He is still in the league playing for the Los Angeles Lakers looking to bring back another title with the help from other star players around the league.
